What is a Raspberry Pi?

A Raspberry Pi is a low cost, credit-card sized computer. It is capable of doing everything a desktop computer to do such as browsing the internet, making programs, word-processing, and playing games. There are different models ranging from around $5 to $35. The Raspberry Pi runs Linux (a variety of distributions), and is open source!

What you will learn

In this project, you will learn how to get a Raspberry Pi running and find out what it can do! This "Getting Started" page will walk you through the installation steps, show you what the pins are used for and how to read them, and show you what you can do in the Raspberry Pi.
